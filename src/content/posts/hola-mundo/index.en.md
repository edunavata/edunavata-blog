## Why a blog?

I've always believed that the best way to learn something is to try to explain it to someone else. This blog is exactly that: a learning-in-public exercise.

### What you'll find here

I'll write about what interests me:

- Web development and tooling
- Productivity and workflows
- Thoughts on technology

I don't promise a fixed schedule, but I do promise every article will have something useful.

See you around!
